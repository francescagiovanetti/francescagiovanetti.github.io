SIIAM is the Italian Society for Artificial Intelligence in Medicine.
The organization promotes collaboration across medicine, research, and innovation to help artificial intelligence translate into meaningful clinical impact.

Its work brings together clinicians, researchers, entrepreneurs, and institutions interested in the responsible development and application of AI in healthcare.

The English website offers an overview of the society, its mission, and the broader community around AI in medicine in Italy.
