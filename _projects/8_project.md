HealthMakers explores what it actually means to build in healthcare, beyond polished narratives and surface-level innovation talk.

The project focuses on candid conversations about incentives, constraints, execution, and the real stakes involved in translating ideas into meaningful impact in medicine.

Through this work, the goal is to make healthcare innovation more honest, practical, and accessible to people trying to understand or shape the space.
