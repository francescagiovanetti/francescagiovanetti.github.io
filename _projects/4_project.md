Futurely is a platform focused on helping young people navigate their future paths with better access to guidance, mentorship, and opportunity.

Within Futurely, the healthcare track supports learners interested in medicine, innovation, and healthcare-related careers through targeted mentorship and exposure.

This project highlights mentorship work aimed at helping participants better understand the healthcare landscape and the paths available within it.
