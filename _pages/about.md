<h1 style="font-size:4.8rem; font-weight:700; line-height:1; margin-top:0; margin-bottom:28px;">
Francesca Giovanetti
</h1>

<p style="font-size:1.25rem; line-height:1.65; margin-bottom:28px;">
I am a Medical Doctor and Entrepreneur working at the intersection of medicine, innovation, and business.
</p>

<p style="font-size:1.25rem; line-height:1.65; margin-bottom:28px;">
I am currently a Founder’s Associate at <a href="https://syntropicmedical.com">Syntropic Medical</a>, where I collaborate closely with the CEO on business development while supporting the company’s daily operations. Drawing on my medical background, I work with key stakeholders to understand clinical needs and translate them into strong strategic and market perspectives.
</p>

<p style="font-size:1.25rem; line-height:1.65; margin-bottom:28px;">
I graduated summa cum laude in Medicine from the University of Padua, Italy. During my studies, I pursued several international experiences exploring different dimensions of healthcare innovation. My work spans medical research at Airlangga University, venture capital at <a href="https://ninacapital.com">Nina Capital</a>, and startups such as <a href="https://dubtx.com">DUB Therapeutics</a> and Syntropic Medical. These experiences helped me understand how scientific discoveries move from research to real-world healthcare solutions.
</p>

<p style="font-size:1.25rem; line-height:1.65; margin-bottom:28px;">
Alongside my professional work, I actively contribute to global innovation networks including <a href="https://www.highimpactmedicine.org">High-Impact Medicine</a>, the Healthcare Leadership Academy, <a href="https://nucleate.xyz">Nucleate</a>, the world’s largest community of bio-innovators, and the <a href="https://www.siiam.it/en/sign-in">Italian Society for Artificial Intelligence in Medicine (SIIAM)</a>.
</p>

<p style="font-size:1.25rem; line-height:1.65; margin-bottom:0;">
Combining clinical training with entrepreneurial experience, I bring a multidisciplinary perspective to healthcare innovation, focusing on how emerging technologies can translate into meaningful improvements for patients and healthcare systems.
</p>
