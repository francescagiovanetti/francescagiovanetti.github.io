Francesca Giovanetti

I am a Medical Doctor working at the intersection of medicine, innovation, and business.

I am currently Founding Medical Product Manager at You(th), where we are rethinking prevention by turning smartphones into scalable, non-invasive screening tools that assess 60+ biomarkers in minutes.

Previously, I was Founder’s Associate at Syntropic Medical, a neurotech startup developing a wearable technology to treat depression through flickering light-induced neuroplasticity. At Syntropic, I worked closely with the CEO on business development and daily operations, while leveraging my medical background to understand clinical needs and translate them into clear strategic and market insights. Turned a research paper into a medical device deployed in 2 clinical trials in less than 2 years.

I graduated summa cum laude in Medicine from the University of Padua, Italy. After my studies, I pursued several international experiences exploring different dimensions of healthcare innovation, including medical research at Airlangga University, venture capital at Nina Capital, and work with startups such as DUB Therapeutics, Syntropic Medical, and now You(th).

Alongside my professional work, I actively contribute to global innovation networks including High-Impact Medicine, the Healthcare Leadership Academy, Nucleate, the world's largest community of bio-innovators, and the Italian Society for Artificial Intelligence in Medicine (SIIAM).

Combining clinical training with entrepreneurial experience, I bring a multidisciplinary perspective to healthcare innovation, focusing on how emerging technologies can translate into meaningful improvements for patients and healthcare systems.
